Weekly LION Reports

Pick your client below to submit this week's LION report. The bot pulls the numbers — you fill in the story, the issues, and the plan.

How this works

The bot will auto-pull all your weekly metrics (sales, ACoS, TACoS, PPC spend, conversions, organic, etc.) directly from Amazon. You only fill in what the bot can't know:

  • What you actually optimized this week
  • What's blocking performance (stock, ratings, returns, etc.)
  • Inventory status and client context
  • Your plan and expected outcome for next week

Time to fill out: about 5 minutes per client. Submit every Monday morning before standup.
